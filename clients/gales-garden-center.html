Gale's Garden Center has been a beloved fixture in the community — a destination for gardeners, homeowners, and plant enthusiasts of every experience level. With an expansive selection of plants, seasonal décor, and garden essentials, Gale's needed a marketing partner who could bring the warmth and vitality of their brand to life on screen and online.

Perception Multimedia stepped in to produce seasonal video commercials and power a targeted paid media strategy — driving foot traffic and digital engagement through broadcast-quality production and precision digital advertising. From spring planting season to holiday sales, every campaign was designed to meet customers exactly where they were.
