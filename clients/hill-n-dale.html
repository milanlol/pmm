Hill 'N Dale Club is a private sportsman's club tucked into the rolling landscape of Medina, Ohio — a place where members come for the hunt, stay for the camaraderie, and return for a tradition that runs generations deep. The club offers upland hunting, fishing, and clay target shooting across beautifully maintained grounds. Perception Multimedia partnered with Hill 'N Dale to build a complete brand presence: a new website, professional photography, event video coverage, and print materials that honor the club's heritage while giving it a polished, modern voice.

Hill 'N Dale is a club with deep roots and high standards. Every piece we made for them had to earn its place — nothing generic, nothing off-the-shelf. Our photography captured the quiet intensity of upland hunting, the camaraderie of clay shooting, and the beauty of a property that speaks for itself. The website we designed reflects that same balance: refined enough for a private club, functional enough for real members.

The business card design carries the club's warmth into print — a backdrop that feels as natural as the grounds themselves, paired with typography that signals membership in something worth belonging to.
