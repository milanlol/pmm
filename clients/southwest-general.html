Southwest General Hospital is a full-service community hospital serving the southwest suburbs of Cleveland. We partnered with Southwest General across a comprehensive suite of services — from on-site photography and video production capturing their physicians and staff, to website design and digital marketing campaigns that connect the hospital with the patients and community they serve. Our work spans physician spotlights, digital advertising, print collateral, and a website experience built to guide patients with clarity and confidence.

Our Approach

PATIENT

Healthcare marketing demands more than good design — it demands authenticity. Patients are making real decisions about their care, and every piece of content we created for Southwest General was built to earn their trust. Our photography puts faces to names. Our videos humanize the physicians. Our digital marketing delivers the right message at the right moment.

The website redesign unified all of these elements — a clean, navigable digital front door to the hospital system that serves patients across every device and channel. Paired with strategic digital marketing, the result is a cohesive brand presence that connects community members to the care they need.
