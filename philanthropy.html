APPC Annual Ride
2 End Alzheimer's

Play Video

One of our most significant community initiatives is the APPC Annual Ride 2 End Alzheimer's. This charity motorcycle ride brings together riders and supporters to raise funds for the Alzheimer's Association.

The ride brings together motorcycle enthusiasts, supporters, and local businesses for a powerful cause. Riders cover nearly 200 scenic miles through Northeast Ohio before gathering for food, raffles, and a community celebration at the Medina VFW.

Over the years, the event has grown significantly, helping to raise thousands of dollars toward Alzheimer's care, support, and research. It's more than just a ride — it's a way for us to honor loved ones, rally around a shared mission, and give back in a big way. We're proud to lead this annual event and grateful to everyone who rides, donates, or volunteers to make it a success.

Action Door & Window
Christmas Fundraiser

Every year, Action Door & Window organizes their Annual Christmas Fundraiser — and we're proud to be on the ground capturing every moment of it.

One hundred percent of donations collected at this event go directly to those experiencing homelessness in our community. It's a powerful reminder of what's possible when local businesses rally around a shared purpose and put people first.

100% of Donations

Every dollar raised at the Action Door & Window Christmas Fundraiser goes directly to supporting those experiencing homelessness in Northeast Ohio.
